EE - Online Shop

Background

EE's primary objective for their online store was to provide a consistent experience across many platforms and devices. My task was to design high-fidelity prototypes for each device breakpoint during the design and development phase. Creating these prototypes were done in HTML5, CSS3 and javascript

Process

We aimed to meet 3 primary device breakpoints. They were:

  • Mobile (320 x 480 pixels)
  • Tablet (< 768 pixels)
  • Desktop (> 1024 x 768 pixels)

Early prototypes quickly identified capabilties of each device prior to production development, e.g. how video renders on each platform. This would be a constant evolution during the development phase.

High fidelity prototyping has major advantages over classical wireframes when dealing with responsive web design. It removes the need for constant documentation up until the final prototype has been approved. Users are able to see real world examples of how their design would render on a HTC Wildfire mobile device over Apple's Thunderbolt Cinema Display. It would also show the interactive nature and technical limitations of each device.
